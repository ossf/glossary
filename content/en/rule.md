A rule is an active, enforceable [policy](policy.md), regulation, or law.

## Problem it addresses

Organizations need a clear idea of what must be followed and what can be [evaluated](evaluation.md) and [enforced](enforcement.md).
Vague or inactive "rules" make [compliance](compliance.md) and [audit](audit.md) unclear.

## How it helps

Defining a rule as active and enforceable separates what is binding from general [guidance](guidance.md).
It supports [policy](policy.md), [control](control.md), and [compliance](compliance.md) by making obligations explicit.
