Preventive enforcement is any action that stops another process from causing non-compliance before it happens.

## Problem it addresses

Responding only after non-compliance occurs can be costly or too late.
Organizations need ways to block actions that would violate [policy](policy.md) or [control](control.md)s.

## How it helps

Preventive enforcement reduces [risk](risk.md) by interrupting processes that would lead to non-[compliance](compliance.md).
It complements [remediative enforcement](remediative-enforcement.md) and supports a fuller [enforcement](enforcement.md) strategy.

Source: [Gemara: A Governance, Risk, and Compliance Engineering Model for Automated Risk Assessment](https://openssf.org/resources/gemara-a-governance-risk-and-compliance-engineering-model-for-automated-risk-assessment/)
