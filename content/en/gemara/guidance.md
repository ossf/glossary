Guidance is prose meant to help achieve a desired outcome for a topic or general scenario, based on knowledge of relevant [vector](vector.md)s.

## Problem it addresses

Teams need practical direction that is informed by how systems can be misused or neglected.
Generic advice that ignores [vulnerability](vulnerability.md) and [threat](threat.md)s is less useful for security and [risk](risk.md) management.

## How it helps

Guidance that references [vector](vector.md)s helps readers design and operate systems with known risks in mind.
It supports [control](control.md) and [policy](policy.md) design and can be organized in a [catalog](catalog.md) of [guideline](guideline.md)s.

Source: [Gemara: A Governance, Risk, and Compliance Engineering Model for Automated Risk Assessment](https://openssf.org/resources/gemara-a-governance-risk-and-compliance-engineering-model-for-automated-risk-assessment/)
