Compliance is adherence to a [rule](rule.md) or set of rules.

## Problem it addresses

Organizations need a simple way to say whether something meets the requirements they have set.
Without a clear idea of compliance, it is hard to judge the results of [assessment](assessment.md) and [evaluation](evaluation.md).

## How it helps

Defining compliance as adherence to rules gives a shared standard for [evaluation](evaluation.md) and [enforcement](enforcement.md).
It supports [policy](policy.md) and [audit](audit.md) by making it clear what "meets the requirement" means.

Source: [Gemara: A Governance, Risk, and Compliance Engineering Model for Automated Risk Assessment](https://openssf.org/resources/gemara-a-governance-risk-and-compliance-engineering-model-for-automated-risk-assessment/)
